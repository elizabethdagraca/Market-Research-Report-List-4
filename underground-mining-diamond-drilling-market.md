<p><ul><li>Rotary Drilling</li><li>Wireline Drilling</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Underground mining diamond drilling market includes two main types: Rotary drilling and Wireline drilling. Rotary drilling involves the use of a rotating drill bit to create boreholes in the ground, allowing for the extraction of core samples. On the other hand, Wireline drilling utilizes a wireline system to retrieve core samples without removing the entire drill string from the borehole. Both methods are commonly used in exploration and production activities in underground mining operations.</p></p>
