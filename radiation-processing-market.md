<p><p>Radiation processing market can be broadly categorized into two types: isotope radiation processing and accelerator radiation processing. Isotope radiation processing involves using radioactive isotopes to emit ionizing radiation for applications such as sterilization and food preservation. On the other hand, accelerator radiation processing utilizes accelerators to generate high-energy radiation beams for a wide range of industrial and medical applications. Both types of radiation processing offer unique benefits and play a crucial role in various industries.</p></p>
