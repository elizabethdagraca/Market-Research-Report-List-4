<p><strong>The Electronic Personal Radiation Dosimeter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Electronic Personal Radiation Dosimeter</li><li>Passive Electronic Personal Radiation Dosimeter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>An Active Electronic Personal Radiation Dosimeter continuously monitors radiation exposure in real-time, providing instant alerts if radiation levels exceed safe limits. These dosimeters are commonly used in high-risk environments where workers are exposed to radiation on a regular basis. In contrast, Passive Electronic Personal Radiation Dosimeters record radiation levels over a certain period of time and require analysis after exposure. They are typically worn by individuals in low to moderate radiation environments for periodic monitoring of radiation exposure levels.</p></p>
