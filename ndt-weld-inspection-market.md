<p><p>NDT Weld Inspection Market Types include a variety of methods such as Automated Ultrasonic Testing, Magnetic Particle Testing, Radiographic Testing, Penetrant Testing, Visual Testing, and Others. </p><p>Automated Ultrasonic Testing uses high frequency sound waves to detect defects in welds. Magnetic Particle Testing utilizes magnetic fields to identify surface and near-surface defects. Radiographic Testing uses X-rays or gamma rays to examine the internal structure of welds. Penetrant Testing involves applying a liquid dye to reveal surface defects. Visual Testing involves visually inspecting welds for defects. Other methods may include eddy current testing, acoustic emission testing, and leak testing.</p></p>
