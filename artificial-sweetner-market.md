<p><p>The artificial sweetener market comprises various types, including Aspartame, Acesulfame-K, Monosodium Glutamate (MSG), Saccharin, and Sodium Benzoate. MSG is primarily used as a flavor enhancer but is sometimes classified with sweeteners. Saccharin is one of the oldest, offering zero calories, and is commonly used in diet products. Sodium Benzoate is mainly a preservative but can affect sweetness perception in food products.</p></p>
