<p><p>The Telemedicine Stethoscope market includes two main types: Wireless Stethoscopes and Wired Stethoscopes. Wireless Stethoscopes use Bluetooth or WiFi technology to transmit heart and lung sounds to a computer or mobile device for remote diagnosis and consultation. These are convenient for telemedicine appointments where mobility is needed. On the other hand, Wired Stethoscopes are connected directly to a device via a cable, providing a reliable and secure connection for telemedicine consultations, especially in environments where wireless signals may be unreliable.</p></p>
