<p><p>RFID systems are categorized into two main types - Passive RFID System and Active RFID System. Passive RFID systems do not require a power source and rely on the reader's signal to transmit data. They are cost-effective and suitable for short-range applications. On the other hand, Active RFID systems have a power source and can transmit data over longer distances. They are more expensive but offer greater range and capabilities. Both systems are widely used in various industries for tracking and managing inventory and assets.</p></p>
