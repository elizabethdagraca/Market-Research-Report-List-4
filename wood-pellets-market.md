<p><strong>The Wood-Pellets Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>White Pellet</li><li>Black Pellet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wood-pellet market comprises two primary types: white pellets and black pellets. White pellets are typically made from clean, dried wood waste and are used predominantly for residential heating and commercial power generation due to their high energy content and low emissions. In contrast, black pellets are derived from lignin-rich feedstocks, involving a torrefaction process that enhances their energy density and durability, making them suitable for industrial applications and co-firing in coal power plants. Each type caters to specific energy needs and applications.</p></p>
