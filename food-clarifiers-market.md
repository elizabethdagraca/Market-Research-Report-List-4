<p><p>Food clarifiers market can be categorized into two main types: solid separation and solid-liquid separation. Solid separation involves the removal of solid particles from liquids, such as filtering out impurities from juices or removing sediment from beer. On the other hand, solid-liquid separation is the process of separating solids from liquids, such as separating pulp from fruit juice. These methods play a crucial role in ensuring a clean and clear final product for the food and beverage industry.</p></p>
