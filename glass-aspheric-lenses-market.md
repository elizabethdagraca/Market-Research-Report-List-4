<p><p>Glass aspheric lenses are used in various applications such as consumer electronics, healthcare, and automotive industry. There are three main types of glass aspheric lenses in the market: convex lenses, convex concave lenses, and concave lenses. Convex lenses are thicker at the center and thinner at the edges, used for focusing light rays. Convex concave lenses have one convex and one concave surface, used for magnification and reduction. Concave lenses are thinner at the center and thicker at the edges, used for diverging light rays.</p></p>
