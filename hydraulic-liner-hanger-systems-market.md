<p><ul><li>Rotating Type</li><li>Non-Rotating Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hydraulic Liner Hanger Systems are available in two main types: Rotating and Non-Rotating. Rotating Type liner hangers allow for rotation of the liner during installation, improving the cementing process and overall well integrity. Non-Rotating Type liner hangers, on the other hand, do not allow rotation of the liner during installation but still provide reliable anchoring and sealing of the liner in place. Both types cater to different wellbore conditions and operational requirements in the oil and gas industry.</p></p>
