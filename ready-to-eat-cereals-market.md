<p><p>Ready-to-eat cereals market can be divided into two main types - pure cereals and composite cereals. Pure cereals are made from only one type of grain, such as oats or corn. They are popular among consumers looking for simple and natural ingredients. Composite cereals, on the other hand, are made from a combination of different grains, nuts, seeds, and dried fruits. They offer a variety of flavors and textures, appealing to consumers who enjoy a more complex and diverse breakfast option.</p></p>
