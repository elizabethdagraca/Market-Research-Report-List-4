<p><p>Spine surgery market can be broadly classified into two types: Open Spine Surgery and Minimally Invasive Spine Surgery. Open spine surgery involves making a large incision to access and treat the affected area, whereas minimally invasive spine surgery involves using smaller incisions and specialized instruments to perform the procedure with minimal disruption to surrounding tissues. The minimally invasive approach is gaining popularity due to its reduced recovery time, less post-operative pain, and lower risk of complications compared to open spine surgery.</p></p>
