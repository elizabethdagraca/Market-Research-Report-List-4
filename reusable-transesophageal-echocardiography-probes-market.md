The 2D TEE probes provide high-quality two-dimensional images of the heart structures, while the 3D/4D TEE probes offer advanced imaging capabilities by providing three-dimensional or even four-dimensional views of the heart in real-time. Both types of probes are commonly used in cardiology procedures to assess cardiac function, diagnose heart conditions, and guide interventions.
