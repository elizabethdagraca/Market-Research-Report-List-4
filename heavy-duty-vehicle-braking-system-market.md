<p><p>Heavy-duty vehicle braking systems are crucial for maintaining safety on the roads. The market for these systems is typically categorized into two types: Drum Brake and Disc Brake. Drum brakes are more traditional and cost-effective, utilizing brake shoes to press against the drum to create friction and slow down the vehicle. On the other hand, disc brakes are more efficient and provide better stopping power, using brake pads to clamp onto a rotor to slow down the vehicle. Both systems have their own advantages and are widely used in heavy-duty vehicles.</p></p>
