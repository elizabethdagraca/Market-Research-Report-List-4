<p><p>Obstacle Avoidance Sonar is a technology used in underwater vehicles to detect and avoid obstacles in their path. It works by emitting sound waves and measuring the time it takes for the waves to bounce back from objects in the water, giving the vehicle an accurate picture of its surroundings. This technology is crucial for ensuring the safe navigation of autonomous underwater vehicles, submarines, and other underwater equipment.</p><p>The Obstacle Avoidance Sonar Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 8.8% during the forecast period.
