<p><strong>The X-Ray Microscopes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Transmission X-Ray Microscope</li><li>Scanning X-Ray Microscope</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The X-Ray Microscopes market comprises two main types: Transmission X-Ray Microscopes (TXM) and Scanning X-Ray Microscopes (SXM). TXM utilizes transmitted X-rays through a sample, providing detailed images of internal structures and composition at the nanometer scale. In contrast, SXM scans a sample's surface with focused X-rays, generating high-resolution images and mapping elemental compositions. Both technologies are essential in fields such as materials science, biology, and nanotechnology, enabling researchers to analyze materials at unprecedented resolutions.</p></p>
