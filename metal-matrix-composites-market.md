<p><p>Metal-matrix composites market can be broadly categorized into four types based on manufacturing processes: powder metallurgy, liquid-permeable, foundry, and deposition technology. Powder metallurgy involves blending metal powders with reinforcing materials. Liquid-permeable method uses molten metal infiltrated into preform materials. Foundry process combines molten metal with reinforcement materials in molds. Deposition technology utilizes methods like thermal spraying or physical vapor deposition to create metal-matrix composites. Each type offers unique benefits and applications in various industries.</p></p>
