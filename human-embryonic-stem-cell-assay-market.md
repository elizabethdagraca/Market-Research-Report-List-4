<p><p>The Human Embryonic Stem Cell Assay Market includes various types such as Viability/Cytotoxicity, Isolation & Purification, Cell Identification, Proliferation, Differentiation, Function, and Apoptosis. Viability/Cytotoxicity assays assess the health of stem cells, while Isolation & Purification methods isolate and purify stem cells from a sample. Cell Identification techniques help in determining the type of stem cells present. Proliferation assays measure the growth of stem cells, while Differentiation assays assess their ability to develop into specific cell types. Function assays evaluate the functionality of stem cells, and Apoptosis assays detect programmed cell death.</p></p>
