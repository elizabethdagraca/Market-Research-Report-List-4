<p><ul><li>Single Pump</li><li>Double Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Kidney Hemodialysis Equipment Market can be classified into Single Pump and Double Pump types based on the number of pumps used in the equipment. Single Pump systems have one pump that is responsible for delivering the blood through the dialyzer, whereas Double Pump systems use two pumps - one for drawing blood from the patient and the other for returning the filtered blood back to the patient. Both types of equipment play a crucial role in facilitating the process of hemodialysis for patients with kidney failure.</p></p>
