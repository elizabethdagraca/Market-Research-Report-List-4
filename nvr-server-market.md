<p><p>NVR server market can be divided into two types: embedded and PC-based. On the other hand, PC-based NVR servers require a separate computer system to run the software, offering more flexibility and scalability. Both types cater to different needs and requirements of users, with embedded servers being more suitable for smaller installations and PC-based servers providing more advanced features for larger-scale deployments.</p></p>
