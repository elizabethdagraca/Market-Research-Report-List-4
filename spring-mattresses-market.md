<p><p>The spring mattress market features various coil types, each offering distinct support characteristics. Bonnell coils are hourglass-shaped and provide traditional support, while offset coils use a hinged design for enhanced body contouring. Continuous coils consist of a single wire creating interconnected coils, ensuring durability and stability. Marshall coils, or pocketed coils, are individually wrapped to reduce motion transfer and provide personalized comfort. Each coil type caters to diverse consumer preferences, influencing their choice of mattress based on comfort and support needs.</p></p>
