<p><ul><li>PE Tarps</li><li>PVC Tarps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hay Tarps are essential for protecting hay from the elements during storage. Two common types of hay tarps are PE Tarps and PVC Tarps. PE Tarps are made from polyethylene and are lightweight, cost-effective, and easy to handle. They offer protection against UV rays, water, and mildew. PVC Tarps, on the other hand, are made from polyvinyl chloride and are more durable and long-lasting. They provide excellent protection against harsh weather conditions and are tear-resistant. Both types of tarps are widely used in the agriculture industry for hay storage.</p></p>
