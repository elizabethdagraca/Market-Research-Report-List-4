<p><p>PET release liner market is divided into three types based on the coating used on the liner. Silicone coating offers a smooth and low release force surface, making it ideal for applications like labels and tapes. Non-silicone coating provides a good release performance without the use of silicone. No coating market refers to release liners without any additional coating, suitable for applications where no release agent is needed. Each type caters to different industry requirements and applications.</p></p>
