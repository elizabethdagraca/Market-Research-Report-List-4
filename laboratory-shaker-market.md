<p><p>Laboratory shaker market offers various types to suit different scientific and research needs. Vortex shakers are ideal for mixing small vials, while orbital shakers provide gentle mixing and constant speed. Rocking shakers are designed for samples that require a back-and-forth motion. Incubator shakers combine shaking and temperature control for cell culture studies. Other shakers include magnetic stirrers and rotators that also offer precise mixing capabilities for different applications in laboratories. Each type of shaker caters to specific requirements in the scientific community.</p></p>
