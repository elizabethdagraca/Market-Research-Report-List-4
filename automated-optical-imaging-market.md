<p><p>Automated Optical Imaging technologies such as Optical Coherence Tomography (OCT) and Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) are used in medical imaging for detailed visualization of tissues and organs. OCT uses light waves to create high-resolution cross-sectional images of tissues, especially in ophthalmology. MRI, on the other hand, uses magnetic fields and radio waves to produce detailed images of internal organs and structures. Both technologies play a crucial role in diagnosis and monitoring of various medical conditions, contributing to advancements in healthcare.</p></p>
