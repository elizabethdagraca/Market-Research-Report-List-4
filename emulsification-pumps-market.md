In contrast, multi-stage pumps have multiple impellers which can provide higher pressure and better efficiency, making them suitable for more demanding applications. Single-stage pumps are suitable for basic emulsification tasks, while multi-stage pumps are preferred for complex or high-pressure emulsification processes.</p></p>
