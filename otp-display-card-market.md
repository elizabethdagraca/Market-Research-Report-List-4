<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The OTP Display Card Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Event-based (HOTP) Tokens</li><li>Time-based (TOTP) Tokens</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The OTP Display Card market encompasses two main types: Event-based (HOTP) and Time-based (TOTP) tokens. HOTP tokens generate a one-time password based on a cryptographic algorithm and a counter, activating upon each event, making them suitable for systems where passwords are needed intermittently. In contrast, TOTP tokens produce a password that is valid for a specific time frame, usually 30 seconds, enhancing security in environments requiring frequent authentication. Both types are widely used for secure access across diverse applications.</p></p>
