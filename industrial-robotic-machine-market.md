<p><p>The industrial robotic machine market consists of various types of robots designed for different applications. Articulated robots have multiple joints for flexibility in motion. Parallel robots have multiple arms connected to a common base, offering high speeds and precision. SCARA robots are ideal for assembly and pick-and-place tasks. Cylindrical robots are used for handling heavy payloads with high accuracy. Cartesian robots have a rectangular configuration for simple linear motion tasks. Each type of robot caters to specific industrial needs based on its design and functionality.</p></p>
