<p><p>Chairside digital dental materials refer to materials used by dentists in their clinic to create restorations like crowns, bridges, and veneers using CAD/CAM technology. These materials are designed for quick and easy use in a clinical setting, providing efficient and accurate results. Laboratory digital dental materials, on the other hand, are used by dental laboratories to fabricate restorations based on digital impressions sent by dentists. These materials are typically more durable and precise, as they are created in a controlled laboratory environment. Both types play a crucial role in modern digital dentistry.</p></p>
