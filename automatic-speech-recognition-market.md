<p><ul><li>Speaker-Dependent Speech Recognition System</li><li>Speaker-Independent Speech System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automatic Speech Recognition market is divided into two main types: Speaker-Dependent Speech Recognition System and Speaker-Independent Speech Recognition System. </p><p>Speaker-Dependent systems require users to train the system with their voice, leading to higher accuracy for that specific user. On the other hand, Speaker-Independent systems do not require individual training and can recognize the speech of any user, making them more versatile but potentially less accurate. Both types are widely used in various industries for applications such as virtual assistants, dictation software, and customer service automation.</p></p>
