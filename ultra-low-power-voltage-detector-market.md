<p><strong>The Ultra Low Power Voltage Detector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>The Ultra Low Power Voltage Detector market comprises three primary types: N-channel, P-channel, and CMOS. N-channel detectors use an N-type transistor to sense voltage levels, providing high speed and low power consumption. P-channel detectors employ P-type transistors, offering complementary benefits such as lower gate drive requirements. CMOS detectors utilize complementary pairs of N-channel and P-channel transistors, resulting in extremely low power consumption and high noise immunity. Each type caters to specific applications in battery-operated devices, IoT, and low-power electronics.</p></p>
