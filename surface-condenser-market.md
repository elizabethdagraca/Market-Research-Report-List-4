<p><strong>The Surface Condenser Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Central Flow Surface Condenser</li><li>Regenerative Surface Condenser</li><li>Evaporative Surface Condenser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Surface condensers are used in various industries to convert steam back into water for re-use. The central flow surface condenser has a single flow path for the cooling water, making it simple and cost-effective. Regenerative surface condensers use a series of heat exchangers to improve efficiency by preheating the feedwater. Evaporative surface condensers use both air and water to cool the steam, allowing for a smaller footprint and reduced water usage. Each type of surface condenser offers unique benefits for different applications.</p></p>
