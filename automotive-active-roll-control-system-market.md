<p><p>Automotive Active Roll Control System aims to reduce body roll and improve the stability of vehicles during cornering. Electric Active Roll Control (EARC) systems use electric actuators to independently control the suspension system, while Hydraulic Active Roll Control (HARC) systems use hydraulic fluid to achieve similar results. EARC systems are known for their quicker response times and more precise control, while HARC systems are praised for their reliability and durability. Both technologies offer improved handling and driving comfort, making them popular in high-performance and luxury vehicles.</p></p>
