Very Low Frequency detectors are versatile and can easily differentiate between different types of metals. Beat-frequency Oscillation detectors are commonly used for detecting small metal contaminants in packaged foods. Each type has its own unique features and benefits catering to the specific needs of the frozen food industry.</p></p>
