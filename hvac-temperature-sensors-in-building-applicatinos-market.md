<p><strong>The HVAC Temperature Sensors in Building Applicatinos Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>NTC</li><li>PTC</li><li>RTD</li><li>Thermocouples (TCs)</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>HVAC temperature sensors in building applications include various types: NTC (Negative Temperature Coefficient) sensors, which have resistance that decreases as temperature rises; PTC (Positive Temperature Coefficient) sensors, whose resistance increases with temperature; RTDs (Resistance Temperature Detectors), offering high accuracy and stability through metal resistance changes; and thermocouples (TCs), which generate voltage based on temperature differences at junctions of different metals. Other sensors may include infrared and semiconductor-based options, each serving unique roles in maintaining optimal indoor climate conditions.</p></p>
