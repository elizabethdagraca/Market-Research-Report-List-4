<p><p>The hemodynamic monitors market is categorized into three main types: non-invasive, minimally invasive, and invasive monitors. Non-invasive monitors use external sensors to assess blood flow and pressure without breaking the skin. Minimally invasive monitors involve small sensors inserted via catheters, providing detailed information with less risk than full surgical procedures. Invasive monitors require direct access to the vascular system, offering precise hemodynamic data but with higher risks and complications. Each type serves distinct clinical needs based on patient conditions.</p></p>
