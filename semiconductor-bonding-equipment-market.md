<p><strong>The Semiconductor Bonding Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wire Bonder</li><li>Die Bonder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Semiconductor bonding equipment market can be categorized into two main types: wire bonder and die bonder. Wire bonder is used to connect semiconductor devices with tiny wires, ensuring proper electrical connectivity. On the other hand, die bonder is used to precisely place semiconductor chips onto a substrate. Both types of bonding equipment are crucial in the semiconductor industry for ensuring the reliable performance of electronic devices. The demand for these machines is driven by the increasing complexity and miniaturization of semiconductor components.</p></p>
