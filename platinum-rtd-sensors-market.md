<p><strong>The Platinum RTD Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wire-Wound RTD</li><li>Thin Film RTD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Platinum RTD sensors are commonly categorized into two main types: Wire-Wound RTD and Thin Film RTD. Wire-Wound RTDs consist of a platinum wire wound around a core, providing high accuracy and stability in temperature measurements. In contrast, Thin Film RTDs use a thin film of platinum deposited on a substrate, offering faster response times and better long-term stability. Both types are widely used in industries where precise and reliable temperature sensing is required, such as aerospace, automotive, and industrial manufacturing.</p></p>
