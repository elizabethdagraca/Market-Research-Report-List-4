<p><p>The TCPP Flame Retardant Market includes various types such as Endothermic Degradation, Dilution of Gas Phase, Gas Phase Radical Quenching, and Thermal Shielding. Endothermic Degradation involves the absorption of heat during the degradation process. Dilution of Gas Phase works by reducing the concentration of flammable gases. Gas Phase Radical Quenching interrupts the chain reaction of combustion in the gas phase. Thermal Shielding provides a protective barrier that lowers the temperature of the material. Each type offers unique mechanisms to prevent or slow down the spread of fire.</p></p>
