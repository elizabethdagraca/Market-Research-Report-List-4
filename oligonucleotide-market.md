<p><p>Oligonucleotide market is divided into two main types: DNA and RNA. DNA oligonucleotides are short sequences of nucleotides that are complementary to a specific DNA target, often used in molecular biology research and genetic testing. RNA oligonucleotides, on the other hand, are short sequences of nucleotides that specifically target and interact with RNA molecules, used in RNA interference (RNAi) technology and therapeutic applications. Both DNA and RNA oligonucleotides play crucial roles in various scientific and medical fields.</p></p>
