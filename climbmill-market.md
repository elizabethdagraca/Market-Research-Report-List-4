<p><p>Climbmills can be categorized into two main market types: Electromagnetic Climbers and Non-electromagnetic Climbers. Electromagnetic Climbers use magnets to create resistance, providing a smooth and quiet workout experience. They are often pricier but offer more advanced features like programmable workouts. Non-electromagnetic Climbers use mechanical resistance systems like air or friction to provide resistance. While generally more affordable, they may not offer as many advanced features but still provide a great workout experience for those looking for a more basic option.</p></p>
