<p><p>Inductors in the power line market come in various types, each serving specific applications. Winding inductors utilize coils of wire, offering high inductance and efficiency. Laminated inductors feature thin sheets of magnetic material to reduce losses and improve performance. Film sheet inductors are made by layering thin films, providing compact designs and precise inductance. Braided inductors consist of braided wire, enhancing flexibility and thermal performance. Each type caters to different power requirements, contributing to the efficiency and reliability of electrical systems.</p></p>
