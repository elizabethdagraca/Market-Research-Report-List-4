On-pump transfusion devices are used during procedures where the heart is stopped and a heart-lung machine is used, while off-pump transfusion devices are used during procedures where the heart continues to beat without the use of a heart-lung machine. Both types of devices help in minimizing the risks associated with blood transfusions and improve patient outcomes.</p></p>
