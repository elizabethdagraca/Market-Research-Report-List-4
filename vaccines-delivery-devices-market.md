<p><p>Intranasal delivery devices are used for administering vaccines through the nose, offering a painless and needle-free option. Both types of devices are gaining popularity in the vaccines delivery market for their efficiency and ease of use. Intranasal devices are commonly used for flu vaccines, while intradermal devices are used for vaccines like the influenza, HPV, and polio. Both offer promising alternatives to traditional injection methods.</p></p>
