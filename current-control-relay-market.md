DIN Rail Mounting relays are designed to be easily mounted on standard DIN rails, commonly used in industrial applications. Panel-mount relays, on the other hand, are mounted directly onto control panels or cabinets, providing a convenient and space-saving solution for various electrical control systems. Both types offer reliable and efficient control of current flow in electrical circuits, catering to different installation requirements and preferences in the market.</p></p>
