<p><p>The 3D Interactive Lens and Assemblies Market includes two main types of technologies: Structured Light Method and Time of Flight Method (ToF). Structured Light Method uses patterned light to capture depth information and create 3D models, commonly used in industrial applications for precision measurement. Time of Flight Method measures the time it takes for a light pulse to reflect off an object and back to the sensor to calculate distance, often used in consumer electronics like smartphones for facial recognition and augmented reality features.</p></p>
