<p><p>The integrated stove market features two primary types: Deep Well and Side Suction types. The Deep Well type involves a recessed cooking area that facilitates even heat distribution, allowing for versatile cooking methods. In contrast, the Side Suction type is designed with a lateral exhaust system that effectively captures smoke and odors directly from the cooking surface, enhancing indoor air quality. Both types cater to modern culinary needs, combining functionality and design to optimize kitchen space and cooking efficiency.</p></p>
