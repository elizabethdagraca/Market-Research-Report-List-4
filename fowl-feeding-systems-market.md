<p><p>The fowl feeding systems market is divided into three main types: rotary feeding system, conveyor belt feeding system, and self-running feed system. The rotary feeding system involves a rotating mechanism that distributes feed to the birds in a circular motion. The conveyor belt feeding system uses a moving belt to transport feed to the designated feeding areas. The self-running feed system operates autonomously, utilizing sensors and timers to dispense feed at regular intervals without manual intervention. Each system provides a different approach to efficiently distribute feed to fowl.</p></p>
