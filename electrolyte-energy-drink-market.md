<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Electrolyte Energy Drink Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Isotonic</li><li>Hypotonic</li><li>Hypertonic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electrolyte energy drinks come in three main types based on their concentration levels relative to the human body: isotonic, hypotonic, and hypertonic. Isotonic drinks have a similar concentration to bodily fluids, making them effective for hydration and replenishing electrolytes during moderate exercise. Hypotonic drinks have a lower concentration, making them ideal for fast hydration and replacing lost fluids. Hypertonic drinks have a higher concentration, providing more energy but may be slower to absorb, making them better suited for post-exercise recovery.</p></p>
