<p><p>High Performance Liquid Chromatography (HPLC) market includes various types such as Partition Chromatography, Normal-phase Chromatography, Displacement Chromatography, Reversed-phase Chromatography (RPC), Size-exclusion Chromatography, Ion-exchange Chromatography, and Bioaffinity Chromatography. Partition Chromatography separates compounds based on their distribution between two immiscible phases. Normal-phase Chromatography separates compounds based on polarity. Reversed-phase Chromatography separates compounds based on hydrophobic interactions. Size-exclusion Chromatography separates compounds based on size. Ion-exchange Chromatography separates compounds based on charge. Bioaffinity Chromatography separates compounds based on specific biological interactions.</p></p>
