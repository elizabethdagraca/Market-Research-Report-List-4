<p><p>Flat metal detectors can be categorized into two main types - Veneer Type and Segmented Type markets. Veneer Type detectors are made of a single continuous piece of metal, providing a smooth surface for detection. Segmented Type detectors, on the other hand, are made of multiple smaller pieces of metal that are separated into segments, allowing for greater flexibility and precision in detecting metal objects. Both types have their unique advantages and are used in various industries for metal detection purposes.</p></p>
