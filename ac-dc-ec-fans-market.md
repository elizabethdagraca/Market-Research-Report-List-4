<p><p>AC-DC-EC Fans market comprises different types like Axial Fans, Diagonal Fans, Centrifugal Fans, Tangential Fans, and Others. Axial Fans move air parallel to the shaft and are most commonly used for cooling in electronic devices. Diagonal Fans have a mixed airflow direction, while Centrifugal Fans create airflow perpendicular to the fan shaft. Tangential Fans produce airflow across the entire length of the fan blade. Other types of fans could include mixed flow fans, cross flow fans, and propeller fans, each with unique airflow characteristics.</p></p>
