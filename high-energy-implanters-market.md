<p><p>High Energy Implanters are used in semiconductor manufacturing for introducing dopants into silicon wafers to modify their electrical properties. There are two main types of High Energy Implanters - Single Wafer Type and Batch Type. </p><p>Single Wafer Type Implanters process one wafer at a time, providing high precision and control over the doping process. Batch Type Implanters, on the other hand, process multiple wafers simultaneously, offering higher throughput but slightly less precision compared to single wafer type implanters. Both types have their own advantages depending on the specific requirements of the semiconductor manufacturing process.</p></p>
