<p><p>Industrial Cleaning Systems can be categorized into two main types: Wet Cleaning and Dry Cleaning. Wet Cleaning involves the use of water and cleaning solutions to remove dirt, grease, and contaminants from various industrial surfaces. This method is effective for deep cleaning and sanitizing. Dry Cleaning, on the other hand, utilizes absorbent materials and detergents to clean surfaces without the need for water. This method is preferred for delicate surfaces or where water may cause damage. Each type has its own advantages and applications in the industrial cleaning market.</p></p>
