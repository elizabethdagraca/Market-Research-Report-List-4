<p><strong>The Pectinase Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Protopectinases</li><li>Polygalacturonases</li><li>Pectin lyases</li><li>Pectinesterase</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pectinase market is segmented into four main types: protopectinases, polygalacturonases, pectin lyases, and pectinesterases. Protopectinases break down protopectin into soluble pectin, enhancing fruit juice extraction. Polygalacturonases further degrade pectin, improving the clarity and yield of juices. Pectin lyases facilitate gelation processes in food products, while pectinesterases modify the degree of pectin esterification, affecting texture and taste. Each enzyme's unique function drives its demand across food, beverage, and agricultural industries, reflecting evolving consumer preferences and technological advancements.</p></p>
