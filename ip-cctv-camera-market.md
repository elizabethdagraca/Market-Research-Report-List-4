<p><ul><li>Centralized IP Cameras</li><li>Decentralized IP Cameras</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Centralized IP cameras require a central Network Video Recorder (NVR) to manage and record footage from all cameras in the system. This type of system is commonly used in larger or more complex environments where centralized control is needed. </p><p>Decentralized IP cameras, on the other hand, operate independently and have build-in recording capabilities, eliminating the need for a central NVR. These cameras are typically used in smaller installations or in situations where a more distributed surveillance system is preferred.</p></p>
