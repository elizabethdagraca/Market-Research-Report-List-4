<p><p>Manual balancing valves are crucial in hydronic systems, ensuring proper water flow and pressure balance. In the market, two primary types based on material stand out: copper and iron. Copper valves are favored for their corrosion resistance, lightweight nature, and thermal conductivity, making them ideal for smaller, high-efficiency systems. Iron valves, typically heavier and more robust, offer durability and are suitable for larger industrial applications. Both types play significant roles in optimizing system performance and energy efficiency.</p></p>
