<p><p>Fine Powder Activated Carbon market can be classified into Physical Method and Chemical Method types. The Physical Method involves the activation of carbon through processes like steam activation or carbonization, resulting in high surface area and porous structure. On the other hand, Chemical Method involves the activation of carbon using chemical agents like phosphoric acid or zinc chloride, leading to specific surface modification. Both types of activated carbon are widely used in various applications such as water treatment, air purification, and gas adsorption due to their enhanced adsorption capacity.</p></p>
