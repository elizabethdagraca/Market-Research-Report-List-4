<p><p>Biosolids are a byproduct of wastewater treatment processes and can be categorized into two main markets: Industrial Wastewater and Municipal Wastewater. Industrial Wastewater biosolids are generated from industrial activities such as manufacturing and chemical processing and are used for land application or energy generation. Municipal Wastewater biosolids come from household and commercial waste treatment and are typically used for agricultural fertilization or soil conditioning. Both markets aim to manage and reuse biosolids in an environmentally sustainable manner.</p></p>
