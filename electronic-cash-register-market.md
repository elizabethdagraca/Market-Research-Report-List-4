<p><p>Electronic cash registers come in two main types: stationary and portable. Stationary registers are typically found in traditional retail stores, restaurants, and businesses that have a fixed location. They are larger in size and are designed to stay in one place for extended periods of time. Portable registers, on the other hand, are smaller and more lightweight, making them ideal for mobile businesses, event vendors, and pop-up shops. They can be easily carried from one location to another, providing flexibility and convenience for businesses on the go.</p></p>
