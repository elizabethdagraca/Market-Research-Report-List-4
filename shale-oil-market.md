<p><strong>The Shale Oil Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hydrotreating Shale Oil</li><li>No-hydrotreating Shale Oil</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The shale oil market can be divided into two main types: hydrotreating shale oil and no-hydrotreating shale oil. Hydrotreating shale oil involves a process that removes impurities and enhances the oil's quality, making it suitable for refining into cleaner fuels. In contrast, no-hydrotreating shale oil refers to crude oil extraction without this refining step, resulting in products that may contain more contaminants and require additional processing. Each type caters to different market needs and environmental regulations.</p></p>
