<p><ul><li>Wheel Spoke Sensor</li><li>Torque Transducer</li><li>Hole Sensor</li><li>S-Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Capacitive vehicle load sensors come in various types tailored to specific applications. Wheel spoke sensors are designed to measure load on individual wheels, providing real-time data for optimal performance. Torque transducers measure the twisting force on a component, such as a shaft or axle. Hole sensors are used to detect load changes in specific areas of a vehicle. S-Sensors are specialized sensors designed to measure load distribution across various sections of a vehicle, aiding in weight management and efficiency.</p></p>
