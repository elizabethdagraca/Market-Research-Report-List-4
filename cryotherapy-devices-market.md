<p><p>Cryotherapy devices are designed for various purposes, including cryosurgery devices used for targeted tissue destruction, localized cryotherapy devices for specific body areas, and cryochambers for whole-body cryotherapy treatment. Cryosurgery devices use extreme cold to remove abnormal tissue, while localized cryotherapy devices target smaller areas for pain relief or inflammation reduction. Cryochambers, on the other hand, expose the entire body to low temperatures for overall health benefits. These different types of cryotherapy devices cater to a range of medical and wellness needs.</p></p>
