<p><p>Industrial power modules are electronic devices used to provide power in industrial applications. There are two main types of industrial power modules: DC and AC. DC power modules convert alternating current (AC) input into direct current (DC) output, while AC power modules do the opposite, converting DC input into AC output. DC power modules are commonly used in applications such as computers, telecommunications, and automotive systems, while AC power modules are essential for powering machinery and equipment in manufacturing and industrial settings. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring reliable power supply in industrial environments.</p></p>
