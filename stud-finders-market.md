<p><p>Stud finders are tools used to locate studs behind walls before hanging objects. There are two main types in the market: Magnetic stud finders and electronic stud finders. Magnetic stud finders work by detecting metal fasteners in the wall, while electronic stud finders use sensors to locate changes in wall density. Both types have their own advantages and are popular choices among consumers based on their preferences and needs. Magnetic stud finders are often considered more affordable and easier to use, while electronic stud finders are known for their accuracy and additional features.</p></p>
