<p><p>Cable Modem Termination System (CMTS) is a crucial component in cable broadband networks. There are two main types of CMTS markets: Integrated CMTS (I-CMTS) and Modular CMTS (M-CMTS). I-CMTS combines the functions of a CMTS and edge QAM into a single device, simplifying network architecture and reducing space requirements. On the other hand, M-CMTS separates these functions into individual modules, allowing for scalability and flexibility in network expansion. Both types offer distinct advantages depending on the specific needs of the service provider.</p></p>
