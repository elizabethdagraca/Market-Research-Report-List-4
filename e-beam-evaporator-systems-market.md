Multi-pocket e-beam Sources are equipped with multiple pockets for simultaneous deposition of different materials, allowing for versatility in thin film deposition. On the other hand, Single Pocket e-beam Sources have a single pocket for deposition, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness for applications that require deposition of a single material. Both types cater to different needs and preferences in the market for E-Beam Evaporator Systems.</p></p>
