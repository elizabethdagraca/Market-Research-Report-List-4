<p><ul><li>Non-Scanning LiDAR</li><li>Scanning LiDAR</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Optical sensors for LiDAR are categorized into two main types: non-scanning LiDAR and scanning LiDAR. Non-scanning LiDAR systems utilize a fixed field of view to capture the surrounding environment, making them simpler and more cost-effective. On the other hand, scanning LiDAR systems employ a rotating mechanism to sweep the laser beam across the field of view, allowing for more detailed and accurate 3D mapping of the surroundings. Both types of LiDAR sensors play a crucial role in various industries, including autonomous vehicles, agriculture, and urban planning.</p></p>
