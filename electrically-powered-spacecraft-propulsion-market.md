<p><p>Electrically Powered Spacecraft Propulsion market consists of various types such as Hall Effect Thruster (HET), Pulsed Plasma Thruster (PPT), and others. HETs utilize electric and magnetic fields to accelerate ions and generate thrust, providing efficient propulsion for long-duration missions. PPTs use electrodes to create plasma pulses that propel the spacecraft forward. Other types of electric propulsion systems include Ion Engines, Magnetoplasmadynamic Thrusters, and Electrodynamic Tethers. Each type offers unique benefits and capabilities for different space missions.</p></p>
