<p><p>Water-cooled centrifugal chillers are mechanical refrigeration systems that use water as a primary source of cooling. These chillers are commonly used in commercial and industrial applications to cool large buildings, data centers, and other facilities. They work by transferring heat from the building to the chilled water loop, which is then circulated back to the chiller to be cooled.</p><p>The global water-cooled centrifugal chillers market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.5% during the forecast period. This growth can be attributed to the increasing demand for energy-efficient cooling systems, stringent government regulations on energy consumption, and the growing need for sustainable cooling solutions. Additionally, advancements in technology, such as the integration of IoT and automation in chiller systems, are driving market growth.</p><p>The latest trends in the water-cooled centrifugal chillers market include the development of environmentally friendly refrigerants, the adoption of smart chiller systems with remote monitoring capabilities, and the increasing popularity of modular chillers for scalability and efficiency.
