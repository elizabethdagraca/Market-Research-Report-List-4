<p><p>Labyrinth compressors are categorized based on the number of stages they possess in the compression process. Single-stage compressors have one stage where the gas is compressed, suitable for low-pressure applications. Two-stage compressors have two stages for higher compression ratios and are used for medium-pressure applications. Multistage compressors have multiple stages, providing even higher compression ratios for high-pressure applications. Each type is designed to meet specific requirements in various industries based on the level of compression needed.</p></p>
