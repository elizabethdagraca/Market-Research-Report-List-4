<p><p>Laser measurement sensors are classified into various types based on their technology and applications. Digital laser sensors use pulses of laser light to measure distance and are commonly used in industrial automation. CMOS laser sensors use a complementary metal–oxide–semiconductor image sensor to capture light and are often used in robotics and consumer electronics. Other sensor types in the market include ultrasonic sensors, laser triangulation sensors, and laser displacement sensors, each offering unique advantages for specific measurement needs.</p></p>
