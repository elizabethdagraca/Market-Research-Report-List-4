<p><p>Manual cold roll laminators are operated by hand, requiring the user to push the materials through the machine to apply the laminate. These are ideal for small scale projects and are more affordable than automatic laminators. Automatic cold roll laminators, on the other hand, are powered by electricity and can laminate materials automatically without the need for manual intervention. They are suitable for large scale projects and can provide faster and more consistent results.</p></p>
