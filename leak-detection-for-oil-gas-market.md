<p><p>Leak detection in the oil & gas market can be achieved through various methods such as flow meters, infra-red detection systems, and fiber optic sensors. Flow meters measure the flow rate of the substance, which can indicate a leak if there is an unexpected decrease in flow. Infra-red detection systems use sensors to detect hydrocarbon vapors in the air, signaling a potential leak. Fiber optic sensors utilize light signals to detect changes in pressure or temperature, which can indicate a leak in the pipeline.</p></p>
