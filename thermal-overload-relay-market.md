<p><p>Thermal Overload Relays are safety devices used to protect electrical circuits from overheating. There are two main types of Thermal Overload Relays - Melting Alloy Thermal Overload Relay and Bimetallic Strip Thermal Overload Relay. </p><p>Melting Alloy Thermal Overload Relays operate by using a special metal alloy that melts when the current exceeds a certain threshold, breaking the circuit. </p><p>Bimetallic Strip Thermal Overload Relays utilize two different metals with different coefficients of thermal expansion, causing the strip to bend and break the circuit when heated by excess current.</p></p>
