<p><p>Submerged Arc Welding (SAW) pipes are commonly available in two types - Spiral Submerged Arc Welding (SSAW) pipes and Longitudinal Submerged Arc Welding (LSAW) pipes. SSAW pipes are manufactured using a spiral welding process, making them suitable for high-pressure applications. On the other hand, LSAW pipes are produced using a longitudinal welding process, providing better dimensional accuracy and high-strength properties. Both types of SAW pipes are widely used in applications such as oil and gas transportation, construction, and water transmission due to their durability and reliability.</p></p>
