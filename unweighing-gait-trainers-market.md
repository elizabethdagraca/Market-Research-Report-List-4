<p><p>Unweighing Gait Trainers are devices used in physical therapy to assist individuals with walking difficulties. They come in three main types - manual, electric, and pneumatic. Manual trainers require the therapist to manually adjust the amount of weight support provided. Electric trainers use powered systems to adjust weight support automatically. Pneumatic trainers utilize air pressure to control the amount of unweighing support. Each type offers unique benefits and cater to different needs, ensuring a variety of options for healthcare providers and patients.</p></p>
