<p><p>Active Protection System (APS) includes both Soft Kill System (SKS) and Hard Kill System (HKS) markets. SKS are designed to interfere with or deceive incoming threats, such as guided missiles, through methods like jamming or decoying. On the other hand, HKS physically intercepts and destroys the threat before it reaches its target. Both systems have their unique capabilities and are widely used by military forces around the world to enhance the protection of vehicles, such as tanks, against various threats on the battlefield.</p></p>
