<p><p>Ambulatory Electrocardiography (ECG) market consists of three main types of devices: Resting ECG Devices, Stress ECG Devices, and Holter Monitors. Resting ECG Devices are used for routine cardiac monitoring, Stress ECG Devices are used during exercise to assess heart function under stress, and Holter Monitors are portable devices worn by patients for continuous monitoring of heart activity over a period of time. These devices play a crucial role in diagnosing and monitoring cardiovascular conditions outside of healthcare facilities.</p></p>
