<p><ul><li>Absorptive Mufflers</li><li>Reactive Mufflers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive mufflers can be classified into two main types: absorptive mufflers and reactive mufflers. Absorptive mufflers work by absorbing sound waves through sound-deadening materials like fiberglass or metal wool, while reactive mufflers use specifically designed chambers and tubes to reflect and cancel out sound waves. Absorptive mufflers are known for their effective noise reduction capabilities, while reactive mufflers are popular for their efficient back pressure regulation. Both types play a crucial role in reducing noise pollution and improving overall vehicle performance in the automotive market.</p></p>
