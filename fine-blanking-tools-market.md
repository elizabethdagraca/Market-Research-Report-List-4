<p><strong>The Fine Blanking Tools Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sliding-punch Fine Blanking Tools</li><li>Fixed-punch Fine Blanking Tools</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fine blanking tools are categorized into two main types: sliding-punch fine blanking tools and fixed-punch fine blanking tools. Sliding-punch tools utilize a moving punch that slides along the surface of the workpiece, allowing for more precise and complex cuts. On the other hand, fixed-punch tools have a stationary punch that remains in a fixed position during the cutting process. Each type of tool offers unique advantages and is used in various applications within the fine blanking industry.</p></p>
