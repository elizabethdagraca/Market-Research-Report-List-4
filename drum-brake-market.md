<p><ul><li>Leading Trailing Shoe Brake</li><li>Dual Two Trailing Shoe Brake</li><li>Dual Two Leading Shoe Brake</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The drum brake market features several types of designs catering to various vehicle needs. The leading-trailing shoe brake uses one shoe as leading during braking, enhancing effectiveness and stability. The dual two trailing shoe brake employs two trailing shoes, optimizing braking force in specific applications where rear braking is prioritized. Conversely, the dual two leading shoe brake consists of two leading shoes, offering better braking performance and stability under heavy loads, making it ideal for performance and commercial vehicles.</p></p>
