<p><p>Drilling motors are classified into two main types based on the method of operation: dry drilling motors and wet drilling motors. Dry drilling motors are designed to operate without the use of drilling fluid, making them ideal for projects where minimal fluid is preferred. On the other hand, wet drilling motors are specifically designed to work with drilling fluid, allowing for increased efficiency and performance in projects where fluid is necessary. Both types of drilling motors cater to different needs and preferences in the drilling industry.</p></p>
