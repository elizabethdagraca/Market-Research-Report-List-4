<p><p>Oily sludge treatment systems are classified based on their methods of operation. Dehydration type systems use thermal or chemical processes to separate water from the sludge, reducing its volume and facilitating disposal. Centrifugal type systems rely on centrifugal force to separate solids and liquids in the sludge, allowing for easy removal of water and oil. Both types of systems are commonly used in industries such as oil and gas, petrochemicals, and manufacturing to effectively treat and manage oily sludge waste.</p></p>
