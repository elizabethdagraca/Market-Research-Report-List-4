<p><p>Fiber Optical Interconnects market includes cable assemblies, connectors, silicon photonics systems, PIC-based interconnects, optical engines, optical transceivers, free space optics, fibers & wave guides. Cable assemblies are used to connect fiber optic cables, while connectors provide a way to join cables or devices. Silicon photonics systems utilize silicon-based technology for data transmission. Photonic integrated circuits (PICs) enable advanced interconnect solutions. Optical engines combine lasers and detectors for high-speed data transmission. Optical transceivers convert electrical signals into optical signals for transmission. Free space optics use air as a medium for optical communication. Fibers & wave guides guide and transmit light signals in fiber optics technology.</p></p>
