<p><p>NGS-based diagnostics market includes various types such as DNA sequencing, RNA sequencing, whole genome sequencing, whole exome sequencing, and other sequencing applications. DNA sequencing involves analyzing the sequence of nucleotides in DNA samples, while RNA sequencing determines the sequence and abundance of different RNA molecules. Whole genome sequencing maps out the entire DNA sequence of an organism, while whole exome sequencing focuses on the protein-coding regions. Other sequencing applications can include metagenomics, epigenomics, and targeted sequencing for specific genetic mutations or biomarkers.</p></p>
