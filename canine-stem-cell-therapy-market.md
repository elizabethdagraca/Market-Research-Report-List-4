<p><p>Canine stem cell therapy market includes two main types: allogeneic stem cells and autologous stem cells. Allogeneic stem cells are sourced from a different dog of the same species, while autologous stem cells are derived from the patient's own body. Allogeneic stem cells are often preferred for off-the-shelf treatments, offering a potentially quicker and easier solution for patients. On the other hand, autologous stem cells are seen as a personalized treatment option, as they are tailor-made for each individual dog's specific needs.</p></p>
