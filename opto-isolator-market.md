<p><p>Opto-isolators are electronic components that provide electrical isolation between input and output circuits using light. There are two main types of opto-isolators: non-linear and linear. Non-linear opto-isolators are used for applications where digital signals need to be isolated, such as in power supplies and motor control systems. Linear opto-isolators, on the other hand, are used for applications where analog signals need to be isolated, such as in audio equipment and sensor interfaces. Both types provide critical isolation for reliable and safe operation of electronic systems.</p></p>
