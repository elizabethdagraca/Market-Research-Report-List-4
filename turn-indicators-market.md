<p><p>Turn indicators are essential automotive components signaling a driver’s intention to turn or change lanes. The market is divided into electronic and mechanical types. Electronic turn indicators utilize LED technology, offering faster response times and improved visibility, often integrated with modern vehicle systems. In contrast, mechanical turn indicators use traditional filament bulbs and rely on relay mechanisms, providing a familiar, albeit less efficient, signaling method. The choice between these types often depends on vehicle design, cost, and desired technology integration.</p></p>
