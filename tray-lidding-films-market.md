<p><p>Tray lidding films are available in various types based on the material used. Polyethylene (PE) films are cost-effective and provide good seal strength. Polypropylene (PP) films offer high clarity and barrier properties. Ethylene Vinyl Alcohol (EVOH) films provide excellent oxygen barrier properties. Polyethylene terephthalate (PET) films have good rigidity and heat resistance. Other types of films may include polystyrene, PVC, and aluminum. Each material has its own advantages and is suitable for different packaging requirements in the food industry.</p></p>
