<p><p>Food enzymes market is segmented into various types based on their function, including carbohydrase, protease, lipase, and others. Carbohydrase enzymes help in breaking down carbohydrates into sugars, while protease enzymes assist in protein breakdown. Lipase enzymes work on fats and oils, aiding in digestion and absorption. Other enzymes in the market may include phytase, lactase, and pectinase, each serving specific functions in food processing and production. Overall, these enzymes play a vital role in enhancing food quality, texture, and nutritional value.</p></p>
