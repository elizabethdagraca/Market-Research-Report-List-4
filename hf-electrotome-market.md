<p><p>The HF Electrotome market consists of two main types: Unipolar Type and Bipolar Type. In the Unipolar Type, the current flows from a single electrosurgical electrode to the patient's body, allowing for precise cutting or coagulation. On the other hand, the Bipolar Type involves the use of two electrodes close to each other, which reduces the risk of unintended tissue damage. Both types of electrotomes are essential tools in various medical procedures, providing surgeons with the ability to perform accurate and controlled tissue dissection and coagulation.</p></p>
