<p><p>Canola oil is available in two main market types: Cold-pressed Canola Oil and Extracted Canola Oil. Cold-pressed Canola Oil is produced by mechanically pressing the seeds without the use of heat or chemicals, resulting in a product with a lighter color and flavor. Extracted Canola Oil, on the other hand, is produced using high heat and chemicals to extract the oil from the seeds, resulting in a more neutral flavor and higher smoke point. Both types are popular choices in the cooking oil market.</p></p>
