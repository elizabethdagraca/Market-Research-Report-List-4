<p><p>Adhesive resin market types include water-based, solvent-based, hot melt, reactive, and others. Water-based adhesives are eco-friendly and offer good adhesion to various surfaces. Solvent-based adhesives have strong bonding capabilities and fast curing times. Hot melt adhesives are solid at room temperature and melt when heated, providing quick bonding. Reactive adhesives require a chemical reaction to cure, offering high performance and durability. Other types include pressure-sensitive adhesives and UV-curable adhesives, each with unique characteristics and applications.</p></p>
