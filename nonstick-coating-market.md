<p><p>The nonstick coating market includes three main types: FEP (Fluorinated Ethylene Propylene), PTFE (Polytetrafluoroethylene), and Ceramic. FEP coatings offer high chemical resistance and low friction properties. PTFE coatings are known for their exceptional nonstick and heat resistance capabilities. Ceramic coatings are popular for being environmentally friendly and scratch-resistant. Each type of coating has its unique characteristics, making them suitable for various applications in the cookware, automotive, and industrial sectors. These coatings provide a reliable solution for preventing sticking and enhancing the lifespan of products.</p></p>
