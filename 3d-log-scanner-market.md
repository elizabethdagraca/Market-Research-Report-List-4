<p><p>The 3D Log Scanner market can be divided into two main types, Laser and X-Ray scanners. Laser scanners use laser technology to create 3D images of logs, measuring dimensions and detecting defects with high accuracy. X-Ray scanners, on the other hand, use X-ray technology to penetrate the log and create detailed internal images, making them ideal for detecting hidden defects. Both types of scanners are used in the forestry industry to optimize log sorting, maximize wood yield, and ensure product quality.</p></p>
