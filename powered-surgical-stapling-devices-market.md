<p><p>Powered surgical stapling devices are widely used in various surgical procedures for quick and efficient wound closure. These devices come in three main types: straight, curved, and circular. Straight staplers are used for linear or straight-line stapling, curved staplers are designed for stapling in tight or curved anatomical spaces, and circular staplers are used for creating circular anastomoses. Each type serves different purposes in surgery, providing surgeons with versatile tools for precise and effective tissue closure.</p></p>
