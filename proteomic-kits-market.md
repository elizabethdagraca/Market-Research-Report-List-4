<p><p>Proteomic Kits Market includes three main types: Extraction Kits, Analysis Kits, and Sequencing Kits. Extraction Kits are used to isolate specific proteins from a sample, Analysis Kits help identify and quantify proteins present in a sample through techniques like Western Blotting or ELISA, and Sequencing Kits enable researchers to determine the amino acid sequence of a protein. These kits are essential tools for studying protein structure and function, biomarker discovery, and drug development in the field of proteomics.</p></p>
