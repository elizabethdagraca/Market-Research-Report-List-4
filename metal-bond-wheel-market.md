<p><p>The Metal Bond Wheel market is segmented into four main types: Resin Bond, Vitrified Bond, Metal Bond, and Plating Bond. Resin Bond wheels are made of synthetic resin and offer good cutting ability. Vitrified Bond wheels are made of vitrified materials and are known for their high precision grinding. Metal Bond wheels are made of metal and are ideal for grinding hard materials. Plating Bond wheels have a thin layer of abrasive material electroplated onto them for precision grinding applications.</p></p>
