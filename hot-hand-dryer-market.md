<p><p>Hand-in hot hand dryers are designed for users to insert their hands into the machine to dry them. This type of hot hand dryer is commonly found in public restrooms and commercial settings. This type of hot hand dryer is often more hygienic as it eliminates the need for direct contact with the machine. Both types of hot hand dryers serve the same purpose of quickly and efficiently drying hands.</p></p>
