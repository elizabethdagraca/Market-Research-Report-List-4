<p><p>Thermally Driven Heat Pump market types include Vertical Closed Loop, Horizontal Closed Loop, Open Loop and Other market. Vertical Closed Loop systems circulate a heat transfer fluid through a series of vertical loops buried deep underground. Horizontal Closed Loop systems involve horizontal loops buried in trenches. Open Loop systems use water from a nearby water source as a heat exchange fluid. Other market types may include hybrid systems or innovative designs that optimize efficiency and sustainability. Each type of system effectively harnesses thermal energy for heating and cooling purposes.</p></p>
