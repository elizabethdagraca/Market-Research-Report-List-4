<p><ul><li>Electromagnetic Flowmeters</li><li>Vortex Flowmeters</li><li>Coriolis Mass Flowmeters</li><li>Ultrasonic Flowmeter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Chemical flow meters are used to measure the flow rate of chemicals in various industrial processes. There are several types of chemical flow meters available, including electromagnetic flowmeters, vortex flowmeters, Coriolis mass flowmeters, and ultrasonic flowmeters. Electromagnetic flowmeters measure flow rate by applying a magnetic field and measuring the voltage generated as the liquid passes through. Vortex flowmeters use the principle of creating vortices to measure flow rate. Coriolis mass flowmeters detect flow rate by measuring the mass of the fluid moving through the meter. Ultrasonic flowmeters use ultrasonic pulses to measure flow rate.</p></p>
