<p><strong>The Medical Adhesion Barrier Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Film Formulation</li><li>Gel Formulation</li><li>Liquid Formulation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Medical adhesion barriers are used to prevent the formation of scar tissue after surgeries. Film formulation barriers are thin sheets that are placed over tissues to prevent adhesions. Gel formulation barriers are thicker substances that are applied in gel form to create a physical barrier between tissues. Liquid formulation barriers are fluid substances that are sprayed or poured onto tissues. Each type of barrier has its own advantages and is used based on the specific needs of the surgical procedure.</p></p>
