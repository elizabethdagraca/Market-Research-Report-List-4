On the other hand, digital radiography equipment uses sensors to capture images electronically, allowing for immediate viewing and analysis on a computer screen. Both types of equipment are used in the industrial radiography market for non-destructive testing of materials in various industries, with digital radiography gaining popularity due to its efficiency and convenience.</p></p>
