<p><p>Compressor impeller market types can be classified into three main categories - Open Style, Semi-open Style, and Closed Style. Open Style impellers have a design with open vanes, allowing for higher flow rates and lower efficiency. Semi-open Style impellers have partially enclosed vanes, balancing between flow rate and efficiency. Closed Style impellers have fully enclosed vanes, offering the highest efficiency but lower flow rates. Each market type caters to different industrial needs and applications based on the desired balance between flow rate and efficiency.</p></p>
