<p><ul><li>Clincher Bike Tyres</li><li>Tubular Bike Tyres</li><li>Tubeless Bike Tyres</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Road bike tyres come in three main types - clincher, tubular, and tubeless. Clincher tyres are the most common and feature a separate inner tube inside the tyre that hooks onto the rim. Tubular tyres are sewn into a casing and then glued onto the rim, providing a smooth ride but requiring more maintenance. Tubeless tyres don't require an inner tube and instead use a sealant to prevent punctures, offering a smoother ride and better grip. Each type caters to different riders' preferences and needs.</p></p>
