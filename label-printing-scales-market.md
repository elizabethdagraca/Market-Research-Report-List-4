<p><p>Label printing scales come in two market types: Double Monitor and Single Monitor. Double Monitor scales have two screens, allowing both the operator and the customer to view information simultaneously. This makes transactions more efficient and convenient. On the other hand, Single Monitor scales have only one screen, typically facing the operator. While not as suitable for customer viewing, Single Monitor scales are often more compact and cost-effective. Both types offer accurate weighing and printing of labels for various industries.</p></p>
