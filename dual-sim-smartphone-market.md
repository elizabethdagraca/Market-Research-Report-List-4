<p><p>The Dual SIM smartphone market features two primary types: Dual Standby Mobile Phones and Dual Active Mobile Phones. Dual Standby Mobile Phones allow both SIM cards to be active but only one can make or receive calls at a time; the other is on standby. Conversely, Dual Active Mobile Phones enable both SIM cards to operate simultaneously, allowing users to receive calls on either line without interruption. This flexibility caters to users needing distinct personal and professional communication channels.</p></p>
