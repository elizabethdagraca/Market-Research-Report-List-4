<p><p>High Speed Type Biophotonic Sensors are classified into two main types based on their design and functionality: Two-Dimensional Sensors and Three-Dimensional Sensors. Two-Dimensional Sensors capture biophotonic signals from a flat surface, providing a detailed image of biological samples. On the other hand, Three-Dimensional Sensors capture signals from multiple depths within a sample, allowing for a more comprehensive analysis of its structure and composition. Both sensor types play a crucial role in various applications such as medical imaging, diagnostics, and biotechnology research.</p></p>
