<p><p>The Silver Paste market is divided into two types: Front Side Silver Paste and Back Side Silver Paste. Front Side Silver Paste is used for front side metallization in solar cells to facilitate the collection of generated electricity. It is crucial for efficient energy conversion. On the other hand, Back Side Silver Paste is used to create a reflective surface on the backside of solar cells to prevent the escape of generated electricity. Both types of Silver Paste are essential components in the production of high-quality solar panels.</p></p>
