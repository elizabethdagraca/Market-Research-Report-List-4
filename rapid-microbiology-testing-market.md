<p><p>Rapid microbiology testing market is classified into different types based on the methods used for detecting and identifying microorganisms. Growth-based methods involve measuring changes in microbial growth in response to specific conditions. Viability-based methods determine the viability of microorganisms by assessing their metabolic activity. Cellular component-based methods focus on detecting specific cellular components of microorganisms. Nucleic acid-based methods detect microbial DNA or RNA. Other methods include immunoassays and biosensors for rapid detection of microorganisms. Each type offers unique advantages for different applications in microbiology testing.</p></p>
