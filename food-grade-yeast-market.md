<p><p>Food Grade Yeast Market consists of two main types: Fresh Yeast and Dry Yeast. Fresh Yeast is comprised of single-celled yeast organisms that are typically sold in a moist form and have a short shelf life. Dry Yeast, on the other hand, is deactivated and comes in a powder or granulated form, allowing for longer storage and ease of use. Both types of yeast are commonly used in baking and fermentation processes to leaven bread, brew beer, and make other food products.</p></p>
