<p><p>Contraband detectors come in two main types - fixed and portable. Fixed contraband detectors are installed in specific locations such as entrances and exits of buildings, prisons, and border checkpoints. They provide continuous monitoring and are designed for high-volume screening. On the other hand, portable contraband detectors are handheld devices that can be easily moved around and used in various locations. They are more versatile and convenient for on-the-go screening purposes, such as in customs inspections or temporary security checkpoints.</p></p>
