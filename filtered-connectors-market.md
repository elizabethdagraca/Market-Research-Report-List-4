<p><strong>The Filtered Connectors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Pass</li><li>High Pass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Filtered connectors are categorized into Low Pass and High Pass types based on the frequency range they allow to pass through. Low Pass filtered connectors are designed to allow low-frequency signals to pass through while blocking high-frequency signals. They are commonly used in applications where only lower frequencies are desired, such as power supplies and audio equipment. In contrast, High Pass filtered connectors allow high-frequency signals to pass through while blocking low-frequency signals. They are commonly used in applications where only higher frequencies are needed, such as in telecommunications and radio communications.</p></p>
