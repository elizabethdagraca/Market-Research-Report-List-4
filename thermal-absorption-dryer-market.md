<p><p>Thermal absorption dryers can be classified into two main types: Intermittent and Continuous. Intermittent dryers operate in batches, where the drying process is stopped and started periodically. This type is suitable for applications where a consistent level of dryness is not critical. Continuous dryers, on the other hand, operate continuously without interruptions, ensuring a steady output of dried products. This type is commonly used in industries where a constant supply of dried materials is required.</p></p>
