<p><p>The surgical nitrile gloves market is divided into two main types: powdered and non-powdered gloves. Powdered gloves are coated with a corn starch or polymer powder to ease donning and removal, enhancing user comfort, but may pose risks of allergic reactions or contamination. Non-powdered gloves, on the other hand, are designed without powder, minimizing the risk of allergic responses, and are preferred in sterile environments. Both types serve essential roles in ensuring safety and hygiene in surgical settings.</p></p>
